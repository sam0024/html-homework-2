The Infinity Castle


The Infinity Castle in "Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba" is a crucial location in the series, particularly during the final arcs. The Infinity Castle has a unique, labyrinthine structure that seems to defy the laws of space and time. It constantly shifts and changes, making it incredibly difficult for intruders to navigate. It serves as the primary base for Muzan Kibutsuji, the main antagonist. This is where he conducts his operations and houses many of the powerful demons.
